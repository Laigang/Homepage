I am an assistant professor at Beijing Normal University in Beijing, China since Sept. 2021. I was previously a postdoctoral fellow working with Prof. Xiao-Shan Gao at AMSS-CAS from Jul. 2019 to Sept. 2021, and also jointly worked with Prof. Raymond W. Yeung at INC-CUHK from Mar. 2021 to Sept. 2021. I received my doctor's degree of applied mathematics under the supervision of Prof. Yufu Chen at UCAS and Prof. Pei Yu at UWO in Jul. 2019.

My research interests are symbolic computation and its applications in inofrmation theory, nonlinear systems, bifurcation theory etc..

Currently, I am focusing on the applications of symbolic computation in proving information theoretic inequalities and identities.

Please feel free to send me an email to discuss potential collaborations.
