Person biography

Laigang Guo received his Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics from UCAS in 2019. Now, he is an assistant professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Beijing Normal University. He has also been a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Institute of Network Coding at CUHK for two years, and in the National Center for Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences at CAS for two years, respectively. His research interests include symbolic computation, information theory, large language models, network coding, and complex systems.

Undergoing projects

Algebraic characterization of specific probability distributions, such as CMIs and Markov Fields, and so on.
